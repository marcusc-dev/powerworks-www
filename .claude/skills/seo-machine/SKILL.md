# SEO Machine Skill

A comprehensive content creation and SEO optimization system for producing high-quality, search-engine-friendly long-form articles.

## Purpose

Streamline the research, writing, analysis, and optimization workflow for businesses seeking to produce professional blog content that ranks well and engages readers.

## Core Capabilities

### Research Phase
Before writing any content, conduct thorough research:
- **Keyword Research**: Identify primary keywords, search volume, difficulty, variations, related questions, and search intent
- **Competitive Analysis**: Review top 10 SERP results to benchmark content length, uncover common themes, and spot content gaps
- **Content Planning**: Develop outline structure, word count targets, supporting evidence, and internal linking strategy

### Writing Standards
Produce long-form articles (2000-3000+ words) with:
- H1 headline containing primary keyword
- Introduction (150-200 words) with hook and problem statement
- Main body (1800-2500+ words) organized into 4-7 H2 sections
- Conclusion with key takeaways and clear CTA
- Natural keyword integration at 1-2% density
- 3-5 internal links and 2-3 external authority links

### SEO Optimization Checklist

**Keyword Optimization**:
- Primary keyword in H1, first 100 words, at least one H2, body content, and conclusion
- Semantic variations throughout
- No keyword stuffing

**Content Structure**:
- Proper heading hierarchy (H1 > H2 > H3)
- Short paragraphs (2-4 sentences)
- Sentences under 25 words average
- 8th-10th grade reading level

**Technical SEO**:
- Meta title: 50-60 characters with primary keyword
- Meta description: 150-160 characters with compelling copy
- URL slug: Short, keyword-rich, hyphenated
- Image alt text: Descriptive and relevant
- Featured snippet opportunities identified

**Link Strategy**:
- Internal links to relevant site content
- External links to authoritative sources
- Descriptive anchor text (not "click here")

### Quality Analysis

After drafting, analyze content for:
- Search intent alignment
- Keyword clustering and density
- Readability scoring
- Content length vs competitors
- SEO quality rating (0-100)

## Deliverables

### Research Brief
Save as `research/brief-[topic-slug]-[YYYY-MM-DD].md`:
- SEO Foundation (keywords, word count targets)
- Competitive Landscape (top competitors, differentiation)
- Recommended Outline (H1-H3 hierarchy)
- Supporting Elements (statistics, quotes, examples)
- Internal Linking Strategy
- Meta Elements Preview (title, description, URL slug)

### Article Draft
Save as `drafts/[topic-slug]-[YYYY-MM-DD].md`:
- Complete article with all headings
- Meta title and description
- Suggested internal/external links
- Image placement recommendations

### Optimization Report
Include prioritized recommendations:
1. **Critical Issues**: Must fix before publishing
2. **Quick Wins**: Easy improvements with high impact
3. **Strategic Improvements**: Long-term optimizations

## Key Principles

1. **User-First**: SEO serves readers, not algorithms
2. **Natural Integration**: Keywords should flow naturally in content
3. **Actionable Recommendations**: Provide specific, implementable changes
4. **Brand Voice Consistency**: Maintain the client's tone and style
5. **Data-Driven Decisions**: Base recommendations on competitive analysis
